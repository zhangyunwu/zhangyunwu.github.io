随机过程

随机过程的概念

对于每个时间t∈Tt\in Tt∈T(TTT是某个固定的时间域)，X(t)X(t)X(t)是一随机变量，则这样的随机变量族[X(t),t∈T][X(t),t\in T][X(t),t∈T]称为随机过程(random process)。
如果TTT是离散时间域，则X(t)X(t)X(t)是一随机事件序列。对振动过程离散采样时，得到的就是时间序列。

随机过程的统计特征

随机过程的各个样本在固定时刻ttt取值进行集合平均，得到随机过程的数学期望
E[X(t)]=μ(t)=∫−∞+∞x(t)dF(x,t)=∫−∞+∞x(t)p(x,t)dxE[X(t)]=\mu(t)=\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} x(t) dF(x,t) =\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} x(t)p(x,t) dxE[X(t)]=μ(t)=∫−∞+∞​x(t)dF(x,t)=∫−∞+∞​x(t)p(x,t)dx

其中F(x,t)F(x,t)F(x,t)和p(x,t)p(x,t)p(x,t)分别是X(t)X(t)X(t)的概率分布函数和概率密度函数。

同样地，均方值可表示为
E[X2(t)]=∫−∞+∞x2(t)dF(x,t)=∫−∞+∞x2(t)p(x,t)dxE[X^2(t)]=\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} x^2(t) dF(x,t) =\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} x^2(t)p(x,t) dxE[X2(t)]=∫−∞+∞​x2(t)dF(x,t)=∫−∞+∞​x2(t)p(x,t)dx

方差为
D[X(t)]=σ2(t)=E[(X(t)−μ(t))2]=∫−∞+∞[x(t)−μ(t)]2dF(x,t)=∫−∞+∞[x(t)−μ(t)]2p(x,t)dx\begin{align*} D[X(t)] & = \sigma^2(t) \\ & = E[(X(t)-\mu(t))^2] \\ & = \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} [x(t)-\mu(t)]^2 dF(x,t) \\ & = \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} [x(t)-\mu(t)]^2p(x,t) dx \end{align*}D[X(t)]​=σ2(t)=E[(X(t)−μ(t))2]=∫−∞+∞​[x(t)−μ(t)]2dF(x,t)=∫−∞+∞​[x(t)−μ(t)]2p(x,t)dx​

为了研究一个随机过程X(t)X(t)X(t)在两个不同时刻的值，即随机变量X(t1)X(t_1)X(t1​)、X(t2)X(t_2)X(t2​)的相互依赖关系，定义它的自相关函数(auto-correlation function)
RXX(t1,t2)=E[X(t1)X(t2)]=∫−∞+∞∫−∞+∞x1(t1)x2(t2)dF(x1,t1;x2,t2)=∫−∞+∞∫−∞+∞x1(t1)x2(t2)p(x1,t1;x2,t2)dx1dx2\begin{align*} R_{XX}(t_1,t_2) & = E[X(t_1)X(t_2)] \\ & = \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} x_1(t_1)x_2(t_2) dF(x_1,t_1;x_2,t_2) \\ & = \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} x_1(t_1)x_2(t_2)p(x_1,t_1;x_2,t_2) dx_1dx_2 \end{align*}RXX​(t1​,t2​)​=E[X(t1​)X(t2​)]=∫−∞+∞​∫−∞+∞​x1​(t1​)x2​(t2​)dF(x1​,t1​;x2​,t2​)=∫−∞+∞​∫−∞+∞​x1​(t1​)x2​(t2​)p(x1​,t1​;x2​,t2​)dx1​dx2​​

其中F(x1,t1;x2,t2)F(x_1,t_1;x_2,t_2)F(x1​,t1​;x2​,t2​)和p(x1,t1;x2,t2)p(x_1,t_1;x_2,t_2)p(x1​,t1​;x2​,t2​)分别为随机变量、的联合概率分布函数和联合概率密度函数。

自协方差函数(auto-covariance function)
CXX(t1,t2)=E[(X(t1)−μ(t1))(X(t2)−μ(t2))]=∫−∞+∞∫−∞+∞(x1(t1)−μ(t1))(x2(t2)−μ(t2))dF(x1,t1;x2,t2)=∫−∞+∞∫−∞+∞(x1(t1)−μ(t1))(x2(t2)−μ(t2))p(x1,t1;x2,t2)dx1dx2\begin{align*} C_{XX}(t_1,t_2) & = E[(X(t_1)-\mu(t_1))(X(t_2)-\mu(t_2))] \\ & = \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} (x_1(t_1)-\mu(t_1))(x_2(t_2)-\mu(t_2)) dF(x_1,t_1;x_2,t_2) \\ & = \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} (x_1(t_1)-\mu(t_1))(x_2(t_2)-\mu(t_2))p(x_1,t_1;x_2,t_2) dx_1dx_2 \end{align*}CXX​(t1​,t2​)​=E[(X(t1​)−μ(t1​))(X(t2​)−μ(t2​))]=∫−∞+∞​∫−∞+∞​(x1​(t1​)−μ(t1​))(x2​(t2​)−μ(t2​))dF(x1​,t1​;x2​,t2​)=∫−∞+∞​∫−∞+∞​(x1​(t1​)−μ(t1​))(x2​(t2​)−μ(t2​))p(x1​,t1​;x2​,t2​)dx1​dx2​​

显然
RXX(t,t)=E[X2(t)]R_{XX}(t,t)=E[X^2(t)]RXX​(t,t)=E[X2(t)]

CXX(t,t)=σ2(t)C_{XX}(t,t)=\sigma^2(t)CXX​(t,t)=σ2(t)

CXX(t1,t2)=RXX(t1,t2)−μ(t1)μ(t2)C_{XX}(t_1,t_2)=R_{XX}(t_1,t_2)-\mu(t_1)\mu(t_2)CXX​(t1​,t2​)=RXX​(t1​,t2​)−μ(t1​)μ(t2​)

规格化自协方差函数(normalized auto-covariance function)、自相关系数(auto-correlation coefficient)
ρXX(t1,t2)=CXX(t1,t2)σX(t1)σX(t2)−1≤ρXX(t1,t2)≤1\rho_{XX}(t_1,t_2)=\frac{C_{XX}(t_1,t_2)}{\sigma_X(t_1)\sigma_X(t_2)} \quad -1 \le \rho_{XX}(t_1,t_2) \le 1ρXX​(t1​,t2​)=σX​(t1​)σX​(t2​)CXX​(t1​,t2​)​−1≤ρXX​(t1​,t2​)≤1

为了研究两个随机过程X(t)X(t)X(t)和Y(t)Y(t)Y(t)在不同时刻的值的相互关系，定义互相关函数(cross-correlation function)
RXY(t1,t2)=E[X(t1)Y(t2)]=∫−∞+∞∫−∞+∞x(t1)y(t2)dF(x,t1;y,t2)=∫−∞+∞∫−∞+∞x(t1)y(t2)p(x,t1;y,t2)dxdy\begin{align*} R_{XY}(t_1,t_2) & = E[X(t_1)Y(t_2)] \\ & = \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} x(t_1)y(t_2) dF(x,t_1;y,t_2) \\ & = \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} x(t_1)y(t_2)p(x,t_1;y,t_2) dxdy \end{align*}RXY​(t1​,t2​)​=E[X(t1​)Y(t2​)]=∫−∞+∞​∫−∞+∞​x(t1​)y(t2​)dF(x,t1​;y,t2​)=∫−∞+∞​∫−∞+∞​x(t1​)y(t2​)p(x,t1​;y,t2​)dxdy​

互协方差函数(cross-covariance function)
CXY(t1,t2)=E[(X(t1)−μX(t1))(Y(t2)−μY(t2))]=∫−∞+∞∫−∞+∞(x(t1)−μX(t1))(y(t2)−μY(t2))dF(x,t1;y,t2)=∫−∞+∞∫−∞+∞(x(t1)−μX(t1))(y(t2)−μY(t2))p(x,t1;y,t2)dxdy\begin{align*} C_{XY}(t_1,t_2) & = E[(X(t_1)-\mu_X(t_1))(Y(t_2)-\mu_Y(t_2))] \\ & = \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} (x(t_1)-\mu_X(t_1))(y(t_2)-\mu_Y(t_2)) dF(x,t_1;y,t_2) \\ & = \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} (x(t_1)-\mu_X(t_1))(y(t_2)-\mu_Y(t_2))p(x,t_1;y,t_2) dxdy \\ \end{align*}CXY​(t1​,t2​)​=E[(X(t1​)−μX​(t1​))(Y(t2​)−μY​(t2​))]=∫−∞+∞​∫−∞+∞​(x(t1​)−μX​(t1​))(y(t2​)−μY​(t2​))dF(x,t1​;y,t2​)=∫−∞+∞​∫−∞+∞​(x(t1​)−μX​(t1​))(y(t2​)−μY​(t2​))p(x,t1​;y,t2​)dxdy​

互相关函数和互协方差函数有如下性质
RXY(t1,t2)=RYX(t2,t1)≠RXY(t2,t1)R_{XY}(t_1,t_2)=R_{YX}(t_2,t_1) \neq R_{XY}(t_2,t_1)RXY​(t1​,t2​)=RYX​(t2​,t1​)=RXY​(t2​,t1​)

CXY(t1,t2)=CYX(t2,t1)≠CXY(t2,t1)C_{XY}(t_1,t_2)=C_{YX}(t_2,t_1) \neq C_{XY}(t_2,t_1)CXY​(t1​,t2​)=CYX​(t2​,t1​)=CXY​(t2​,t1​)

CXY(t1,t2)=RXY(t1,t2)−μX(t)μY(t)C_{XY}(t_1,t_2)=R_{XY}(t_1,t_2)-\mu_X(t)\mu_Y(t)CXY​(t1​,t2​)=RXY​(t1​,t2​)−μX​(t)μY​(t)

CYX(t2,t1)=RYX(t2,t1)−μX(t)μY(t)C_{YX}(t_2,t_1)=R_{YX}(t_2,t_1)-\mu_X(t)\mu_Y(t)CYX​(t2​,t1​)=RYX​(t2​,t1​)−μX​(t)μY​(t)

规格化互协方差函数(normalized cross-covariance function)、互相关系数(cross-correlation coefficient)
